What We Do

Today is the age of information technology and we aim to equip our youth with computer programming skills and prepare them for the development of the country. In this regard, our Saylani Mass IT Training Program providing vocational training to more than 75,000 students in Web and Mobile Application Development, CCNA, Graphics Designing, Computer Based Accounting in Karachi, Hyderabad, Faisalabad, Islamabad. Our goal is to develop more than 1 million software developers across the country, which will add about $100 billion annually to Pakistan's economy.

Services

Provided by SAYLANI MASS IT TRAINING

Industry Connections and Internships

SMIT has strong ties with the tech industry, providing students with opportunities to participate in internships and real-world projects. These connections help students gain practical experience, making them more competitive in the job market upon completing their training.

Collaborations

SMIT collaborates with tech giants like Google, Microsoft, and Amazon Web Services (AWS) to provide industry-recognized certifications and training.

Free IT Courses

SMIT offers a wide range of free courses in various fields such as Web Development, Mobile App Development, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Digital Marketing, and more.

Workshops and Seminars

They regularly conduct workshops, seminars, and webinars to keep students updated with the latest trends and technologies in IT.

Career Counseling and Job Placement

SMIT not only provides education but also assists students with career counseling and job placements in the tech industry.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

SMIT provides students with access to modern facilities and equipment, ensuring they gain hands-on experience with the tools they’ll use in the industry.
